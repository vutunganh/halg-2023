Hotel information

There are many hotels in the walking distance to the conference venue. We recommend booking a hotel as soon as possible, since the tourist season can become quite busy in the summer.

To search for a hotel, one can use one of the following services. The links point to the relevant query, i.e. sorted by distance from the venue, if possible.

Nevertheless, the public transportation in Prague is excellent, so staying at a hotel outside of the walking distance to the venue is feasible.

  • AirBnB
  • Google Travel
